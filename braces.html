Braces vs Clear Aligners

Braces use metal or ceramic brackets, while aligners use transparent trays.

Cost in India (2026)

  • Metal Braces: ₹30,000 – ₹60,000
  • Ceramic Braces: ₹50,000 – ₹80,000
  • Clear Aligners: ₹70,000 – ₹2,50,000

Treatment Duration

Typically 12–24 months depending on case complexity.
