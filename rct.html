What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a dental procedure used to remove infected pulp tissue from inside a tooth and save it from extraction.

Is RCT Painful?

Modern RCT is typically painless due to effective anesthesia and rotary instruments.

Cost of RCT in India (2026)

  • Front Tooth: ₹3,000 – ₹6,000
  • Molar Tooth: ₹5,000 – ₹10,000
  • With Crown: ₹8,000 – ₹18,000
