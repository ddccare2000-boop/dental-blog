What Is a Dental Implant?

A dental implant is a titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone to replace a missing tooth.

Cost in India

₹20,000 – ₹60,000 depending on system and location.

Benefits

  • Permanent solution
  • Natural appearance
  • Preserves jawbone
